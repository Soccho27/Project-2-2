Recommended Treatment:

Leaf blight is a fungal disease commonly affecting crops. To treat: 1. Remove and destroy infected leaves to prevent spread. 2. Apply copper-based fungicide every 7-10 days. 3. Ensure proper spacing between plants for adequate air circulation. 4. Water at the base of plants rather than overhead. 5. Rotate crops in the affected area for the next planting season.

Tip: For severe cases, consider consulting with a local agricultural expert.
